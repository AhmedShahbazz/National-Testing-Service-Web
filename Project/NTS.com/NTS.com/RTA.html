Instructions for Test Takers (AMIDEAST/RTA, Dubai UAE Project, Taxi/Bus Drivers)

Registration of Test takers (Taxi/Bus Drivers)

  • NTS accepts AMIDEAST, UAE/RTA duly approved test takers coming through AMIDEAST/Road Transport Authority, Dubai recruitment agencies/consultants only.
  • NTS contacts only these test takers via SMS/Call to submit Test Registration Form and Fee.
  • The TOEIC and WorkFORCE Registration Forms and Test Fee Bank Deposit Slip are available at NTS website.
  • The Test Fee is "NON TRANSFERABLE AND NOT REFUNDABLE".
  • As per agreement the result of the test shall not be available on NTS website, rather for test results of Taxi/Bus Driver please contact your respective recruitment agencies/consultants.
  • For further query or information, please send email to [email protected]

On Test Day

  • The TOEIC and WorkFORCE test taker for the Taxi/Bus Drivers MUST report the test center 30 minutes before the test time start.
  • Test Taker MUST bring the following documents on test day, otherwise will NOT be allowed to sit in the test.
  • ORIGINAL Roll No. slip issued by NTS.
  • ORIGINAL CNIC.
  • ORIGINAL Passport.
  • Mobile phones are NOT allowed in test centers.
  • The test is Online, Computer Based, so no need to bring stationary.
