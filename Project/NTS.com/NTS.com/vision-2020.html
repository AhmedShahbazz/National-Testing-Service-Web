Vision-2025

NTS 10-Year future plan – Vision-2025 is based on NTS past experiences and future market demands. NTS Vision-2025 aims at reaching out to 10 million direct beneficiaries by year 2025. During the 10-year plan, NTS program focus will be:

  • Testing and Assessment (T&A)
  • Network and Partnership Building (NPB)
  • Institutional Capacity Building (ICB)
  • Regional and International Cooperation (RIC)
  • Information and Knowledge Management (IKM)

    • NTS Vision-2025

    NTS Vision-2025 prime focus will remain services for educational institutions, government, corporate, private industry and professions and also the HR organizations. However, international standards test and assessment products and services will be offered on the patterns of TOEFL, TOEIC, SAT (I and II), ACT, GRE (Gen and Subject), GMAT, MCAT, LSAT, WorkFORCE Assessment For Job Fit, etc. while keeping in view local requirements, conditions/environment and education system of the country. NTS aims to administer CBT all over the country through LAN/WAN setup of educational institutions with centrally developed/controlled software but under strict security. In addition, following new products/services will be offered:

  • Assessments from Grade-5 to Professional Career
  • Tests for Physically Challenged People
  • Coalition Building for Quality Education Policy

    • Other Goals under the Vision – 2025 are:

  • Test Item Contributors will be increased up to 5,000
  • Construction of NTS Center and availability of 07 MTC to conduct tests and Training
  • Training of 10,000 staff and partners in various disciplines and skills
  • Partnership enhancement to 1,000 organizations and institutions within Pakistan
  • Networking & information sharing all over Pakistan
  • NTS Support Pool enhancement to 150 Cities of Pakistan
  • Enhancement of capability to conduct paper-based test for 1 million candidates in 150 cities and computer based test facility in 100 cities of Pakistan simultaneously with a provision of 20 more cities
  • Data entry processing capacity enhancement to 100,000 application forms per day
  • 1,000 institutions as test centers on NTS panel
  • Setting-up Data Center
  • Improved security and secrecy through highly secured Printing Press and Copyrights of contents, use of information & knowledge for planning & action programs
  • Research and advice to educational institutions and policy makers for formalization of quality education and assessment policies in the country
